Antibodies

Antibodies are proteins that protect you when an unwanted substance enters your body. Produced by your immune system, antibodies bind to these unwanted substances in order to eliminate them from your system.

When antibodies find their target, they bind to it, which then triggers a cascade of actions that vanquish the invader. Antibodies are part of the so-called "adaptive" immune system, the arm of the immune system that learns to recognize and eliminate specific pathogens, Greene said.

Plasticwares

Plastic labware refers to laboratory equipment made of plastic materials. It includes a wide range of products such as test tubes, petri dishes, pipettes, containers, flasks, and funnels.

Plastic labware is widely used in scientific research and experimentation due to its advantages such as low cost, lightweight, durability, and chemical resistance. Unlike glass labware, plastic labware is less brittle and less likely to break, making it safer to use in a laboratory setting.
