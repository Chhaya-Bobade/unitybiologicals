Scrub Typhus Elisa Kit

Scrub typhus, also known as bush typhus, is a disease caused by bacteria called Orientia tsutsugamushi.

Product information

Human Anti-Scrub Typhus IgM ELISA Kit quantitate Human Scrub Typhus-IgM in samples. An antigen has been pre-coated onto a microplate. Standards or test samples are added to the wells, incubated and then washed. HRP-conjugated anti-Human Scrub Typhus-IgM IgG antibody is then added and incubated. The plate is washed once more and the chromogen solution is then added which HRP catalyzes, generating a blue coloration after incubation. A stop solution is added which generates conversion to yellow color read at 450 nm which is proportional to the amount of analyte bound.
